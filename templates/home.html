    Who is Len Guff?

    Len Guff was just like you. Just trying to get by in the business world. Earning a crust in the everyday office bants, high-fiving the right people, firmly-handshaking the old firm, mingling at huddles, turning out logs at fireside chats, listening to energetic entrepreneurs pass out PowerPoint pearls at public, publicity, press and PR talks.

    But something was grinding away at Guff. These people who claim to have the keys at the keynotes, were pedalling nonsense. No-one from the breakout room to the power breakfast buffet table was making any sense.

    They were just words, words that sounded right, words that fitted into a soundbite. Phrases which were repeated because they encapsulated an idea or appeared to codify a new approach. But under closer scrutiny they signified nothing. These were the entrepreneur’s new clothes and Guff had had enough.

    So Len started to tap these business catchphrases and cliches into his phone. And he began to pick them apart, to understand where they came from and to suggest how they could be made better or binned. The result was an ebook which is still widely available.

    Now it’s time to align with a new paradigm. To truly digitise and digitalise. The website is no longer about Len Guff. This website is about all of us. So the next time someone throws out an empty phrase in the boardroom or the ten-minute standup - it’s time to figuratively make a stand.

    Put it on this site, so through extended ranting, we can make it better. And who knows if we get enough momentum, traction or velocity (or, you know, words) we could turn out a new ebook. Written by all of us. Because we are all Len Guff.
